Our Donate Plan

Donations are given without return consideration.
Your donation will support a family and work to advance childrens rights and equality for girls.

Food for a family

Foods are very important.They lift morale and give a feeling of security in times of stress. Try to include foods that they will enjoy and that are also high in calories and nutrition.

Clothes for a family

By donating your clothes instead, you can help fight landfill, reduce demand for resource-hungry new clothes, and have a real impact in making fashion more sustainable.

Medicine for a family

The main purpose of disaster medicine is to prevent and reduce the negative effects of disasters on public health,ensure that the health system effectively responds during disasters.

Situation and Support

In the last few weeks, millions of people in the Sylhet and Sunamganj districts experienced heavy rains and flash floods, leaving them homeless and without possessions. With homes crushed, objects floating away and people missing, the country is at high alert.

As the health crisis continues to unfold, we are working together to providing healthcare,medication and all necessary items for stranded families.
